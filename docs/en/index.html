Common Questions

What is screenshot verification?

Screenshot verification confirms that a screenshot was captured authentically and has not been tampered with or generated by AI.

How does RealCap prevent AI forgery?

RealCap captures metadata at the moment of screenshot capture, creating an immutable record that proves authenticity and detects AI-generated fakes.

Can RealCap verify existing screenshots?

RealCap verifies screenshots captured through our app or API. Screenshots captured elsewhere cannot be verified retroactively.
