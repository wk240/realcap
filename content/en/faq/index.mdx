        answer: "Screenshot verification confirms that a screenshot was captured authentically and has not been tampered with or generated by AI. RealCap creates an immutable authenticity record at the moment of capture."

        answer: "RealCap captures metadata at the moment of screenshot capture, including device information, timestamp, and capture context. This metadata is encrypted and cannot be retroactively added to AI-generated images."

        answer: "No. RealCap can only verify screenshots captured through our app, browser extension, or API. Screenshots taken before RealCap installation cannot be verified retroactively."

        answer: "RealCap can verify any screenshot captured through our capture system, including bank balance screenshots, income proof screenshots, identity documents, and communication records."
